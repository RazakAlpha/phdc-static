Corporate Statement

Message

Message

PRESIDENT'S VISION

H.E. NANA ADDO DANKWA AKUFFO-ADDO

The vision of the President H.E. Nana Addo Dankwa Akufo-Addo is to develop a modern, diversified, efficient and financially sustainable “energy economy” that will ensure that all Ghanaian homes and industries have access to adequate, reliable, affordable and environmentally-sustainable supply of energy to meet their needs and to support the accelerated growth and development agenda we envisage for the country.

MESSAGE FROM C/O MINISTER OF ENERGY

DR. MATTHEW OPOKU PREMPEH (MP)

In line with the Government’s desire to create the enabling environment for private sector investments, provide decent jobs for Ghanaians and avenues for the growth of indigenous businesses, the Petroleum Hub Development Corporation has been set up.

For us, this is an innovative way to explore Ghana’s unique comparative advantage, including her location at the center of the World, peace and harmony, attractive investment destination and highly skilled and competent human capital. It also provides the opportunity to tap into the benefits of the continental free trade and growing economies in the sub-region. The Ministry will provide the needed policy leadership and guidance to ensure the successful execution of this important mandate, which is dear to the heart of the President.

I am grateful to the Chiefs and people of the Western Nzema Traditional Area and the Western region for making the land available towards this important national investment. We will ensure that the commercial interest of all stakeholders will be balanced with environmental concerns whiles ensuring the national interest will remain supreme .

MESSAGE FROM CEO

CHARLES OWUSU

Since the conception of the President’s vision to establish the Petroleum Hub Development Corporation, key steps have been taken towards the achievement of this vision. The journey ahead may not be easy, but it is also exciting. We are committed to building a competent team to ensure that this vision becomes a reality. It is the belief in the possibility of building a value chain that creates economic linkages and provides business and growth opportunities for Ghanaians that makes it fulfilling.

Ghana has many advantages to create an industry giant in every aspect of the petroleum and petrochemical products and services value chain. Firstly, given Ghana’s central and strategic location along international shipping lines, a guaranteed easy access to the regional markets seems motive enough to hone the development of a Petroleum and Petrochemicals Hub to boost the economy .

Secondly, being a peaceful and a democratic country with well-resourced security agencies, Ghana is an attractive destination for investments. Thirdly, the continuous investment in education has created a skilled workforce and with the necessary training, we will together be able to achieve this vision. Finally, with reliable electricity supply and stable macroeconomic conditions, the country is conducive for investments in strategic areas like the Petroleum Hub.

We are very mindful of the global energy transition and its impact on such an investment. We will therefore lead this Corporation to balance economic, social and the environment in effort to help achieve the Sustainable Development Goals. Environmental impact of the operations in the Hub will be monitored and mitigated, hence, safety and excellence remain our hallmark.

In addition, we are aware of the impact of the Covid-19 pandemic on numerous businesses within the Petroleum and Petrochemical industry. Our team is building a resilient strategy and a business continuity plan to support companies in the Hub to mitigate the impact of future crisis. It is an honour to be of service to this country and the business community.

The development of a Petroleum Hub will complement the progression of the Financial Services and Aviation Hubs concept, while taking advantage of the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA). Our doors are open for business.  

MESSAGE FROM BOARD CHAIRMAN

AWULAE ANNOR ADJAE III

We are fortunate enough to witness the operationalization of the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA). For us, it represents both opportunities and the can-do spirit of the African. It is within the same understanding that the Petroleum Hub was born.

We recognize Ghana’s unique characteristics including location, benign business environment, robust legal system and a well-educated population and we seek to even improve these attributes to attract, retain and grow investments. The Corporation will develop sustainability strategies to ensure effective environment, waste and energy management strategies and continually build community and stakeholder relationships. By doing this, we will ensure that the objectives for profits, environmental management and human development are equally balanced.

The Hub is here to stay, and we recognize that we can only grow with all stakeholders on board.

Who

Who We Are

• To become an industry giant in every aspect of the petroleum and petrochemical products and services value chain.

• To address the needs of the West African sub-region and beyond through research and development of novel technologies to meet the growing petroleum and petrochemical products and services demands of the continent
Mission

• To promote and develop a competitive and enabling environment for investments and economic linkages through the provision of high- class petroleum and petrochemical products and services

• To promote affordable, reliable and cleaner energy that enables human progress.

Vision

To become the Petroleum Hub of choice for refined petroleum products in Africa and beyond.
